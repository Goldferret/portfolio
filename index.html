Costa Rica National Land-Use Visualization System

Built Costa Rica's first national land-use visualization system processing 101K georeferenced forest inventory plots across 7 classification categories. Designed the full ELT pipeline: raw inventory cleaning, 31 engineered spectral/topographic/soil features, and downstream Random Forest classification at ~80% accuracy.

Delivered to USDA Forest Service stakeholders at the Purdue Data Mine Corporate Partners Symposium. First-of-its-kind geospatial dashboard for Costa Rica national inventory.
